From Who You Are to What You Become

Identity isn't something you find — it becomes visible when you start doing things and paying attention to what you're already capable of. Through four developmental phases, students learn to recognize their own extraordinary capabilities, test where those capabilities create impact, and build a clear direction from that foundation. The program develops identity clarity — knowing your direction and being able to act on it — while building the workforce readiness that connects that clarity to career outcomes.

How It Actually Works

Six mechanisms that turn identity exploration into career-ready evidence — each one connected to real people, real data, and real outcomes.

Internal Evidence

Activities Build Proof

Every real-world action, reflection, and project produces evidence of real capabilities. Not a quiz — a portfolio of demonstrated strengths that grows with each phase.

Mentor-Trained AI

Real Expertise, Built In

Cleo is trained on advice from founding_up's network of business owners, chefs, artists, engineers, and filmmakers — professionals who built identity-driven careers. Their real-world wisdom is embedded in every conversation, recommendation, and career insight.

External Evidence

The Market Validates You

Multiple forms of evidence — self-assessed, peer-confirmed, project-verified, educator-confirmed, and employer-verified — are available across every phase. What changes is the student's capacity to interpret and integrate that evidence into a coherent direction.

AI Navigation

Cleo Guides, You Decide

An AI companion that adapts to your journey — surfacing relevant activities, connecting skill gaps to opportunities, and reflecting your progress back to you. A navigator, not a prescriber.

Trust Architecture

Your Privacy Powers It All

You own your data. Employers see only aggregate insights and the awards you choose to share. That trust is what makes employers invest — and their investment enriches your program.

Compounding Growth

Small Steps, Big Identity

Explorer to Investigator to Practitioner to Professional. Each phase builds on the last. By the time you graduate, you know your direction, you can act on it, and you can articulate why.
